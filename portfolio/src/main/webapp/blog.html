Halfway Done With College

I finished my finals for the Spring 2020 semester during the second week of May. One of the classes that I finished was Data Structures and Algorthms. This means that I am done with the introductory computer science class sequence at Penn, which starts with Intro to Computer Science and ends with Data Structures and Algorthms. I'm proud of myself for hitting this milestone, as it is four semesters in the making! I also finished two other computer science courses this semester. I'm happy that, despite the unexpected shift to remote learning in the middle of the semester, I was still able to do well in my classes and enjoy the work that I was doing. However, I do hope we can shift back to in-person learning safely and soon.
